My Travel Journal

A Day in Yellowstone

August 15, 2023

Today was an incredible day exploring Yellowstone National Park. The highlight was definitely witnessing an eruption of Old Faithful. It's so much more impressive in person than in pictures! We also saw herds of bison grazing in the distance, and even caught a glimpse of a lone wolf. The natural beauty of this place is simply breathtaking.

In the afternoon, we took a hike to see the Grand Prismatic Spring. The colors were so vibrant and surreal. It's amazing how nature can create such a masterpiece. I feel so lucky to have experienced this wonderful place.

Yellowstone National Park, USA

Sunset at the Grand Canyon

August 20, 2023

Witnessing sunset at the Grand Canyon was a truly magical experience. The way the colors of the canyon walls changed with the fading light was something I'll never forget. We found a quiet spot away from the crowds, which made the experience even more special.

After the sun set, we stayed a while longer to stargaze. I've never seen so many stars in my life! It was the perfect end to a perfect day.

Grand Canyon National Park, USA
